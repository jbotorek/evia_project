== eVia

The system is created primarly as a school project to PV249 of FI MUNI (www.fi.muni.cz).


eVia presents mini social network where you can share and explore your tourist routes with your friends.
Create and share your favourite places. Organize events or simply browse the most popular spots. Comment on content and
manage new relationships.


Welcome to eVia - It's time to hit the road!
